Powder for days!

Powder season has struck early on the Wasatch Front! After recieving feet of snow over the past week, meteorologists are forecasting an additional foot in the ski areas in Northern and Central Utah, with an additional blast on the way for Sunday. This has lead to powder junkies to dig into thier gear early this year, while building a snow base which will provided a much needed jump start to the season. Local ski bum Randy Richards said "this is the best early season snow in recent memory!" All residents should be relieved to see snowpack and water levels rising from the moisture deposit.
